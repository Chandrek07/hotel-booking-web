The well-manicured green patches in the resort are haven to range of flora and fauna that can be duly appreciated from Swarnika, the multi-cuisine restaurant that serves sumptuous Indian, Chinese and Continental delicacies. If one has to describe Imperial Heights, it is a perfect combination of colonial and traditional Kumaoni architecture. The usage of mud, stone and pine logs are quite evident in the beautiful structure. What makes the resort incredibly splendid are the breathtaking views related to depth of the Valley, grandeur of the imposing peaks and clear sight of well-lit Almora town during the evening.
